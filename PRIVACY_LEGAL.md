# Privacy Policy for Taquería El Huarachón (Huara-Elite) ⚖️🌮

**Effective Date: April 2, 2026**

At Taquería El Huarachón, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data within the Huara-Elite loyalty application.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Profile Info:** Name, email, and birthday (optional for rewards).
- **Transaction History:** Your taco orders and point redemptions.
- **Location Data:** Used strictly for the "¡Estoy Aquí!" check-in feature to verify branch proximity.
- **Device Info:** Push token for notifications and device model for optimization.

## 2. How We Use Your Data
- To manage your Huarachón loyalty tier and points.
- To provide personalized "Promos of the Day" via our **Huara-Marketing AI Agent**.
- To process digital orders through the Sierra POS system.

## 3. Data Protection (Huara-Secure)
We use hardware-backed encryption (Secure Storage) for all authentication tokens. All PII is scrubbed from our debug logs, and sensitive transactions require cryptographic signatures.

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)
- **Access:** You can request a copy of your data at any time.
- **Deletion:** You can purge your entire account and data history via the "Borrar cuenta" feature in the app settings (Right to be Forgotten).
- **Consent:** You can manage location and notification permissions directly in the app.

# Terms of Service 📜🌮

1. **Eligibility:** You must be 13 years or older to use the Huara-Elite app.
2. **Loyalty Points:** Huara-Points have no cash value and are non-transferable.
3. **Anti-Fraud:** Any attempt to spoof location data or manipulate QR codes will result in permanent account suspension.
4. **AI Interaction:** By using the app, you acknowledge that certain features represent interaction with AI agents (e.g., promotional offerings).
